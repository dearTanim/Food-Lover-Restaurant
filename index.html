About us

Welcome to Food Lover Restaurant, your trusted provider of Georgian food services in Dhaka, Bangladesh.
At Food Lover Restaurant, we are dedicated to bringing the authentic flavors and recipes of Georgia to your doorstep. Our skilled chefs use only quality ingredients to create delicious and traditional Georgian cuisine, including Khachapuri, Khinkali, Shashlik, Dolma, Pkhali, and more.
